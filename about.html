From Selenium to AI agents

I'm a Software Development Engineer in Test who has spent 12+ years building, breaking and automating software — and a former QA Lead who loves turning beginners into confident automation engineers.

For years I trained engineers and students on Selenium with Java and Selenium with Python. As the industry shifted, I went all-in on the modern stack: TypeScript + Playwright, and the AI tooling reshaping our craft — Playwright MCP, AI test generation, planning and self-healing agents.

My teaching philosophy is simple: learn by building real things, understand the "why", and pair with AI instead of fearing it. Every session is live, practical and full of the small details that make automation actually hold up in production.
