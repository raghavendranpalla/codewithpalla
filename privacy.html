Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

Learn with Palla ("we", "us") operates the website learnwithpalla.com, which provides information about our test automation training courses, a blog, and a student portal for enrolled learners. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Contact enquiries. When you contact us through the enquiry form, WhatsApp or email, we receive the details you choose to share — typically your name, email address, phone number and message. We use them only to respond to your enquiry and discuss enrollment.
  • Student portal sign-in. Enrolled students sign in to the portal with Google. Through Google Identity Services we receive your name, email address and profile picture. Your email is checked against our enrollment list to grant access to your batch's course content. This profile information is stored locally in your own browser (localStorage) so you stay signed in; it is not stored in a server database by us.
  • Automatically collected data. Like most websites, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may log standard technical data such as IP addresses and browser type for security and operational purposes.

Cookies, local storage and similar technologies

We use browser localStorage to remember portal sign-in state and quiz scores on your device. Third-party services used by this site — such as Google Sign-In, Google Fonts and embedded Google Drive content for enrolled students — may set their own cookies or collect usage data subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners use advertising cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Specifically:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of some third-party vendors' cookies at www.aboutads.info.

Where required by law (for example for visitors from the EEA or UK), a consent message will be shown before advertising cookies are used.

How we use information

  • To respond to enquiries and manage course enrollment.
  • To give enrolled students access to their batch's course materials.
  • To operate, secure and improve the website.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties except the service providers named above, or where the law requires it.

Data retention

Enquiry messages are kept only as long as needed to handle your enquiry and any resulting enrollment. Portal sign-in data lives in your own browser — you can remove it at any time by logging out or clearing your browser storage. Enrollment records (your email on a batch list) are kept for the duration of your course access.

Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you (such as your email on an enrollment list) by contacting us. If you are in a jurisdiction with specific data protection rights (such as the GDPR or India's DPDP Act), we will honour the rights those laws grant you.

Children

Our courses and website are intended for students and working professionals aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Third-party links

The site links to external services such as WhatsApp, LinkedIn and Google Drive. Their handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision; significant changes will be highlighted on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us via the contact page or message us on WhatsApp.
